Hit The JackWatt™ Monthly Jackpot – Official Rules

1. No Purchase Necessary

No purchase, payment, or energy plan switch is required to enter or win. Void where prohibited. By participating, you agree to abide by these rules and all decisions made by IntelliWatt™.

2. Eligibility

Open to legal U.S. residents age 18 or older. Must have a valid email address and mobile number. Employees of IntelliWatt™ or its affiliates are not eligible to win. Entry is subject to verification and may be void if fraudulent or incomplete.

3. How to Enter

There are multiple ways to earn entries in the HitTheJackWatt™ Sweepstakes. No purchase or plan switch is required to enter or win. Void where prohibited.

3.1 Usage-Based Entry (Primary Methods)

You may earn entries by providing your electricity usage data through one of the following free methods:

  • Smart Meter Texas (“SMT”) Connection: If SMT is available for your home, you may connect your SMT account to IntelliWatt™ so that IntelliWatt™ can securely access your electricity usage history. Connecting SMT earns one (1) entry.
  • Manual Usage Upload: If SMT is not available or you prefer not to connect it, you may upload your electricity usage information (for example, a bill PDF, CSV, or Green Button file) through the IntelliWatt™ portal. A qualifying usage upload earns one (1) entry.

Entries earned from SMT connection or manual usage upload remain active only while IntelliWatt™ has electricity usage data for you from the preceding twelve (12) months. If your usage data on file becomes older than twelve (12) months, these entries will expire and will not be included in future drawings unless you reconnect SMT or upload updated usage data.

3.2 Additional Profile-Based Entries

After you have provided usage data as described above, you may earn additional entries by completing optional profile information in your IntelliWatt™ account:

  • Current Plan Information: Providing your current electricity plan information earns one (1) entry.
  • Home Details: Completing your home details (such as square footage, home type, and basic efficiency factors) earns one (1) entry.
  • Appliance Details: Completing your appliance details earns one (1) entry.

Entries earned from current plan information, home details, and appliance details remain active only while IntelliWatt™ has electricity usage data for you from the preceding twelve (12) months. If your usage data on file becomes older than twelve (12) months, these entries will expire and will not be included in future drawings unless you reconnect SMT or upload updated usage data.

3.3 Referral Entries

You may earn referral entries by inviting friends or family members to sign up for IntelliWatt™. For each referred individual who completes the registration process and provides their own electricity usage data (by connecting SMT or uploading usage as described above), you will receive one (1) additional entry. Referral entries do not expire and will remain associated with your account for all future drawings, unless your account is closed or otherwise becomes ineligible.

3.4 Testimonial Entries (Real Customers Only)

If you switch electricity plans through IntelliWatt™ or purchase or complete an energy-related upgrade through Intellipath Solutions LLC (such as solar, HVAC, insulation, or similar services), you may be invited to submit a testimonial about your experience. If you provide a testimonial in accordance with the instructions in the IntelliWatt™ portal, you will receive one (1) testimonial entry. Testimonial entries do not expire and are only available to users who have actually completed a qualifying plan switch or upgrade. Providing a testimonial is optional and is not required to enter or win.

3.5 Alternate Method of Entry (“AMOE”)

To enter the Sweepstakes without providing usage data or completing any IntelliWatt™ platform actions, you may use the Alternate Method of Entry. AMOE submissions are accepted only by mail. During the Sweepstakes Period, hand-print your full name, mailing address, phone number, and email address on a postcard and mail it, with sufficient postage, to:

IntelliWatt Sweepstakes – AMOE Entry
PO Box – TBD (Address will be updated by Intellipath Solutions LLC)

Limit one (1) AMOE entry per person per calendar month. Each eligible AMOE postcard received during a calendar month will count as one (1) entry in the drawing period that includes that month. Only original handwritten postcards sent via U.S. mail are accepted. Emailed entries, digital submissions, photocopies, bulk mailings, and mechanically reproduced entries are not valid and will be rejected.

4. Jackpot Amount

The monthly jackpot increases by $5 for every customer who switches to a commissionable plan using our platform.

Not all energy plans pay us — and that's okay. We still recommend whatever saves you the most from the plans available through our growing provider network (starting with WattBuy) based on our analysis. But only commissionable plans grow the jackpot.

Drawings only occur when the jackpot reaches a minimum of $500. If not, all entries roll over.

  • 100 commissionable switches = $500 jackpot
  • 1,000 commissionable switches = $5,000 jackpot

Note: You don't have to switch plans or pick a commissionable one to enter or win. But they do help grow the prize pool.

5. Drawing Periods

Drawings occur monthly, on or around the 5th of each month. Entry period runs from 12:00 AM CT on the 1st day through 11:59 PM CT on the last day of the month.

6. Winner Selection

One winner is chosen at random from all valid entries that are active at the time of the drawing. Each entry is a separate chance to win. Entries earned from usage data or profile information must remain active pursuant to Section 3 to be included. Referral, testimonial, and AMOE entries remain active unless the account becomes ineligible.

7. Odds of Winning

Odds depend on the total number of eligible entries received and active at the time of the drawing. They change monthly as more users participate or update their information. Maintaining current usage data helps keep usage-based and profile-based entries eligible for inclusion.
Example: 100,000 total active entries = 1 in 100,000 odds per entry.

8. Winner Notification

Winner will be notified via email and/or SMS within 3 business days. Winner must respond within 14 days and complete a digital claim form. Failure to complete the claim form within 14 days will result in forfeiture of the prize. Alternate winners may be selected if unclaimed.

9. Prize Delivery

Prizes are issued via digital payment (PayPal, Venmo, etc.) or mailed check, based on the winner's preference.

10. Additional Terms

By entering, you agree to receive occasional notifications from IntelliWatt™ - not sales pitches, just helpful updates.
We'll only reach out to share ways you may be able to save money, get more entries, or learn about new energy opportunities in your area.
No spam. No pressure. No strings attached.
